About OMNIA

A teaching organization closing a gap in who gets to do philosophy.

Philosophy has spent centuries inside universities. OMNIA exists to put it within reach of older adults — through senior centers, live online sessions, and free resources — and to give them the tools to keep philosophizing on their own.

Our mission

Our mission is to spread philosophy access to seniors everywhere. From live discussion events to online resources, we promote intergenerational learning and communication. More than that, we give seniors the tools to philosophize for themselves. Philosophy is about sharing ideas, and that is what we strive to do at OMNIA philosophy.
